Upcoming Events ​

The EO Dashboard is organising and supporting hands-on workshops and global open competitions, including the NASA Space Apps, the EO Dashboard Hackathon and IGARSS Tutorials, workshops at FOSS4G, and many others.

  • Upcoming tutorial in the Living Planet Symposium 2025 in Vienna on June 22nd! D.03.18 TUTORIAL - From Earth Science to Storytelling with EO Dashboard

News ​

  • 2025-03-10: Survey for AI-based features on EO Dashboard The eodashboard.org project is looking into potential applications of AI and we would love to hear your thoughts and ideas! We have setup a questionnaire here if you find some time to let us pick your brain. Thank you!
  • 2025-02-05: NASA, ESA, and JAXA sign joint declaration to continue their collaboration on EO Dashboard through September 2026 As the project evolves, the agencies are working closely with academic and industry partners to enhance its capabilities. Future plans involve integrating cutting-edge technologies, incorporating data from new science missions like EarthCARE and PACE, supporting new joint scientific research, and launching new outreach efforts (link to declaration).
  • 2024-07-05: EO Dashboard at IGARSS 2024 The joint ESA, NASA and JAXA team will present the tutorial HD-12: Exploring Environmental Changes with EO: a hands-on journey from data analysis to scientific communication with ESA-NASA-JAXA EO Dashboard. https://www.2024.ieeeigarss.org/tutorials.php#tut10
